Welcome to the official site for Perspolis Productions, home to The Scent of Harvest, a character driven film that takes a unique look at human relationships. This is not a love story. It's simply a film about two people from two different parts of the world, two different generations and two separate lives.

The production began on July 22nd, 2001. It will continue for a period of three weeks in the Los Angeles area. Please browse on for more information about the cast, crew and the story. Photos will become available as the production moves on.
