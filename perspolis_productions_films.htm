THE SCENT OF HARVEST      (this film is in post-production)

This is a high quality independent feature film concerning solitude among America's younger generations.   The film blends the humor of different cultural perspectives on human relations with the universality of solitude and the need for family and friends.  The result is a moving tale of a lonely young professional woman who is befriended by an elderly foreign man.

TUESDAY       (this film is in pre--production)
A businessman steps briefly out of his car, and a few moments later finds his car speeding away:  it has been stolen.  As police chase the stolen car, the story within it unfolds:  a father is urgently trying to take his child to the hospital.  The child has been gravely injured and will soon die if not attended to immediately.  This father, who realizes that no other form of transportation will help his child arrive at the Emergency Department on time, resorts to stealing the car in a desperate attempt to save her life.
