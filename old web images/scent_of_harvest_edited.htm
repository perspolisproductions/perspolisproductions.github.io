Welcome to the official site for The Scent of Harvest, a character driven film that takes a unique look at human relationships.   This is not a love story.  It is simply a film about two people from two different parts of the world, two different generations and two separate lives.

Filming began in the Los Angeles area on July 22nd, 2001 and was completed on August 9th.  Editing began on August 13th and a working draft was completed on the 24th.  Please browse the site for more information about the cast, crew, and the story.  Photos will become available as the production progresses.
