Our Company               

Welcome to Perspolis Productions           

 

Perspolis Productions is an independent film production company based in Denver, Colorado.  We present the rare opportunity to combine high-level production quality with independent creative control.  For our first full-length independent feature film, titled "The Scent of Harvest," we are proud to boast the involvement of two very talented Iranian filmmakers whose previous independent films have won international acclaim.

Iranian filmmakers are widely considered to be some of the most talented and fertile artisans in the field today.   Our two filmmakers have over 40 years of combined experience with 35mm film.  In a country where resources can be limited to the extreme, they have repeatedly triumphed using the most basic tools.   Moreover, their involvement in each of their films has entailed embracing tasks far beyond any single job description.  Their successes, which include international festival awards, have been fueled by their deep-seated passion for filmmaking.  They pride themselves in their passion and their ability to create beautiful films under the most difficult circumstances. 

The "The Scent of Harvest" is presently in the post-production stage in Los Angeles.  We are preparing the groundwork for our next film, titled "Tuesday."
